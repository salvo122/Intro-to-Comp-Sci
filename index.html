Luffy is the greatest shonen MC of all time.


Luffy is officially an emperor of the sea so it's officially a race to the One Piece!

Monkey.D. Luffy has recently become a new emperor of the sea after raiding the headquarters of one of the previous emperors of the sea. His name was Kaido the king of beasts. He was world renowned for his amazing strength and was even called the strongest creature in the world! And Luffy along with his friends he made throughout his journey through the Island of Wano defeated Kaido!

Things you need to know

Definitions

Emperor of The Sea: An extremely strong Pirate Captain who has great influence over the entire world.

MC: Main Character

The One Piece: Greatest treasure every found hidden on the last Island on the Grandline.

Grandline: A section of the world that goes around the Earth's circumference that holds the most dangerous islands on the planet.

Shonen: A type of story aim towards young boys. Most of the time focusing on adventure and action.
